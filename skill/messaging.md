# Messaging Rules

## Tone

- concrete
- founder/operator level
- no inflated buzzwords
- no pretending the product is broader than it is

## Positioning checklist

- one-sentence product definition
- user segment
- high-friction problem
- why now
- why Solana matters in this story
- differentiators backed by evidence

## Anti-patterns

- "revolutionary"
- "seamless" without examples
- "for everyone"
- feature list without user context
- pretending MVP is mature infrastructure
